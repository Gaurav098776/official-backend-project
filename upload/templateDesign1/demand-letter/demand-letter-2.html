Annexure (Consequences of Non-Repayment)
Not repaying a loan can have serious consequences.
Find below the potential consequences of non-repayment of a loan:
1. Late fees and penalties: If you miss a payment, you may be charged a late fee. If you continue to miss payments, we may charge additional penalties.
2. Damage to credit score: When you don't repay a loan, it can damage your credit score in all the bureaus (CIBIL, Equifax, Experian & CRIF). A lower credit score can make it difficult to get approved for future loans, credit cards, or even home loans.
3. Legal action: If you default on a loan, we may take legal action against you. This can include a lawsuit, information to employer, or a court order to repay the loan.
4. Collection agency involvement: If we are unable to recover the amount owed, we may hire a collection agency to recover the debt. This can result in multiple phone calls, messages and field visit to your home or office.
5. Failure in background - You may face serious consequences during following processes if your debt remains unpaid & legal action gets initiated:
1. Employee background verification
2. Passport processing
3. Visa processing
Overall, non-repayment of a loan can have serious consequences and
can impact your financial future.
